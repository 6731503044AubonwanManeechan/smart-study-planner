📘 Smart Study Planner

About

Smart Study Planner is a mobile application designed to help students manage their study tasks, organize schedules, and improve productivity efficiently.

Features

📌 Task Management

Add, edit, and delete study tasks easily

✅ Progress Tracking

Mark tasks as completed and track progress

📅 Study Planning

Organize your daily study schedule effectively

Technology

Flutter · Firebase Firestore · Dart

Project Goal

To improve student productivity by providing a simple and effective study planning tool.
